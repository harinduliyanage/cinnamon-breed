About Us

Breed cinnamon tradings is a Sri Lankan Exports Company who are producing, distributing and exporting pure Ceylon cinnamon products, the business was started in 1989 by D.S De Silva as a cinnamon collector. Now it’s operating in exports business Breed Cinnamon is one of the best Cinnamon growers and exporters in Sri Lanka. Now it’s operating in exporting to the other part of the world. Having been involved in the Ceylon Cinnamon trade for more than a 3 decade they come with valuable hands-on experience and knowledge that is certainly important when it’s come with quality and customer satisfaction.

“We are located in the southern part of the Sri lanka. This is the only best Cinnamon growing area in the world” and our core strengths as a true Ceylon cinnamon grower and exporter are the loyal relationships we cultivate with our customers and the continuous investments we make in our employees. It is a matter of great pride for Breed cinnamon tradings that our first customer is still with us today.

Our Vision

To be the leader in providing total solutions for supplying global cinnamon demand.

Our Mission

To build a result oriented, profitable organization that designs value added pure Ceylon cinnamon quality products, which best meet the needs of our customers now and in the future.
