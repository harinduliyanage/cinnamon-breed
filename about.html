STORY OF PURE CEYLON CINNAMON

Ceylon cinnamon is originally and exclusively native to sri Lanka ,it has been well known for thousands of years in the history all over the world as finest cinnamon and only in Ceylon found anywhere on earth, and this Ceylon cinnamon used as spice by human beings as food flavoring, beverage, medicinal herb and even as an embalming agent. Ceylon Cinnamon story sustained with Ceylon Cinnamon being one of the most demanded after commodities during the 16th and 17th centuries and prior to this the Arabs too engaged profusely in the Cinnamon trade, giving Ceylon Cinnamon its prestigious position in the world as the finest Cinnamon anywhere on earth.

We are Breed cinnamon tradings is honestly being proud that to be a market shareholder in the world cinnamon demand and we highly value our pure business to give the authentic cinnamon experience to whoever the cinnamon consumer in the world .

Ceylon Cinnamon also known as true Cinnamon must not be compared with Cassia which looks like Ceylon Cinnamon since its totally different in its content not the appearance. Though related it is not from the same plant and is considered as vastly different foods from both a nutritive and health perspectives. Ceylon Cinnamon (Cinnamomum zeylenicum) is the only true Cinnamon and contains very little naturally occurring Coumarin when compared to Cassia which has relatively larger doses that can be quite toxic. Ceylon Cinnamon can be identified by its smooth texture, sweeter and more delicate flavor, brittleness and multiple layers unlike Cassia with its thick bark that does not have multiple layers.

Ceylon Cinnamon is known to have many medicinal benefits and has been used as part of both eastern and western medicine throughout history. It is known to treat Arthritis, Asthma, Diabetes, Heart disease and many other health conditions.
