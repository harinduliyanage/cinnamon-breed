What's the Ceylon Cinnamon or true cinnamon.

It's made from the inner bark of the Cinnamomum verum tree. Ceylon cinnamon is the expensive, rare, safe and recommended variety over all other varieties including Cassia, Korintje and Saigon. True cinnamon is graded as the fourth most expensive spice in the world. 80%-90% of world’s production of this product belongs to a small Asian country, known as Sri Lanka.
